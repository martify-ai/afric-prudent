Ghana

Explore Ghana
Capital Accra
Language English
Currency Ghanaian Cedi
Best Time Oct-Apr

Ghana, known as the "Gateway to Africa," offers a rich tapestry of cultural heritage, historical significance, and natural beauty. From the vibrant markets of Accra to the historic Cape Coast Castle, Ghana provides authentic experiences that connect visitors with West African traditions and history.

Jamaica

Explore Jamaica
Capital Kingston
Language English
Currency Jamaican Dollar
Best Time Nov-May

Jamaica, the birthplace of reggae music, is a vibrant Caribbean island known for its stunning beaches, lush mountains, and rich cultural heritage. Beyond the resorts, visitors can explore rainforests, cascading waterfalls, and immerse themselves in the island's unique music, food, and warm hospitality.

Popular Destinations in Jamaica
