Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a tour or experience?

You can book directly through our website by visiting the "Book Now" page, or you can contact our bookings team via email at [email protected] or by phone. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and mobile payment options like M-Pesa in select countries. For specific tours, a deposit may be required to secure your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if cancelled 30 days before the scheduled tour. Cancellations within 14-30 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 14 days before the tour are non-refundable. However, policies may vary for specific packages and destinations.

Do you offer custom or private tours?

Yes, we specialize in creating customized tours and experiences for individuals, families, and groups. Contact our team with your requirements, and we'll design a personalized itinerary that meets your interests, timeline, and budget.
